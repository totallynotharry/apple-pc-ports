# apple-pc-ports
Porting PC games to iDevices

# How to play ports.

#### My game is a .love:

- Download your .love to your iDevice, this can be through iCloud from a PC or Mac, or directly from GitHub.
- Download this app: https://love2d-studio.marknoteapp.com/
- On the app, click the plus icon in the top left.
- Click 'Import .love file'
- Choose your .love file from your file explorer.
- On your game list, click the vertical 3 dots at the end of your game's row.
- Hit Run.

#### Not running? / Want it on your home screen?

- On your game list, click the vertical 3 dots at the end of your game's row.
- Click 'Pin to home screen'
- Follow steps on screen.
- Now your game will be on your home screen, click the icon on your home screen and then press the icon again.
- You're in.
